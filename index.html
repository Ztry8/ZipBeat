What ZipBeat can do

Everything you need to
prototype sound fast

4 Oscillator Types

Sine, square, sawtooth, and triangle waves. Dial in frequency and detune for each block on the grid independently.

Grid Sequencer

A 10 row by 1000 column grid. Click to place sound blocks. Sounds in the same column play simultaneously, perfect for chords and layers.

Playhead Transport

A draggable playhead sweeps left to right. Pause mid-sequence, jump to start, and the playhead resets automatically when silence is reached.

Save and Load Projects

Export your grid as a JSON file and reload it any time. Your full composition with every block and every parameter is stored locally.

Export to Audio

Render your sequence to OGG or WebM audio with configurable bitrate. No server, no upload. All processing happens in your browser.

Zero Dependencies

Pure vanilla JS and Web Audio API. No frameworks, no plugins, no account required. Works offline once loaded.
